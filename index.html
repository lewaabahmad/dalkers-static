About Dalkers

Dalkers is a business run and organized by natives of the Washington Heights community. We are dog lovers, responsible business owners, and your friendliest, neighborhood dog walkers. Here are some frequently asked questions:

What is your pricing?

$15.00 per 30-minute dog walk, per dog. $5.00 per additional dog.

Are dogs walked in groups?

Yes. However, we ALWAYS walk a dog by themself until we get to know them. Dogs will never be grouped with more than two other dogs for a maximum total of three dogs per group walk.

What is the pick-up process for dog-walks?

Dogs are usually picked up from the dog owner's home and dog walkers let themselves in using a key provided by the dog owner. We are flexible, however, and are willing to set-up alternate means should they be required.

Do you handle aggressive and un-fixed Dogs?

We will walk all dogs so long as they are not a threat to the dog walker. Please let us know all about your dogs needs!

Do you allow for special needs?

We aim to address all of our dogs' needs! Please tell us how we can best do so!
